# Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) Case Study  
## CareFlow Medicine Management (System C)

This repository presents a group case study evaluating the **CareFlow Medicine Management** clinical decision support system (CDSS), developed by System C and widely deployed across NHS Trusts in England. The work focuses on how CareFlow’s CPOE+CDS supports safe medication prescribing in real clinical workflows.

---

## My Focus
- **System usage and clinical workflow integration**
- **Doctor-facing prescribing interface and CDS alerts**
- **Benefits and real-world use cases in NHS Trusts**
- **Analysis of NHS adoption and usage status**

---

## System Overview
CareFlow Medicine Management is a knowledge-based CDSS embedded within a CPOE system.
It provides:
- Drug–drug and drug–disease interaction alerts
- Allergy and duplicate therapy checks
- Patient-specific dose calculation
- Integration with EPMA and wider EPR workflows

---

## Real-World Impact
Published evaluations report:
- 43% reduction in prescription errors
- 90% reduction in time spent prescribing
- Prevention of duplicate prescriptions
- Improved patient safety through active clinical checking

## NHS Adoption
CareFlow is the leading ePMA supplier in England, deployed across 33 NHS Trusts and over
100 sites, based on NHS Digital reporting.
